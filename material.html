A photobooth of biomaterial

Made of Acid + Starch + Glycerol

Acid contains hydroxyl group (OH) that increase the material's viscosity and reduce water retention.

Starch contains amylose and amylopectin. The glycosidic bonds form the basic structure of the material.

Glycerol acts as plasticizer.

Adhere to human skin

The material was dried in an incubator.

One side is less dried, so it can adhere to human skin tighter, while the other side is dryer, which won't

stick to clothes or anything.

Used as...

1. Can be used to produce nipple covers.

2. Can be used in bras' shoulder straps to help relieve stress on shoulders

Please be careful that:

1. This material cannot be washed.                                                                                    2. The material is not tough enough to prevent any ruptures.
